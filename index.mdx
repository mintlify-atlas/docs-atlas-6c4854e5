DeepSeek V4 Roleplay Instruct is a guide to special control instructions that let you switch the thinking chain style of DeepSeek-V4 during roleplay (角色扮演) sessions. By appending a short instruction string to your first message, you can steer the model's inner `<think>` process between full character immersion and cold analytical planning — without changing your roleplay content at all.

## How it works

Add one instruction string to the end of your **first user message** in a conversation. The model reads its full conversation history on every turn, so the instruction stays active throughout the entire session without you needing to repeat it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a thinking mode">
    Decide whether you want the model to think in-character (角色沉浸) or analytically (纯分析), or leave it on the default automatic mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy the instruction string">
    Copy the ready-made instruction string for your chosen mode from the [Instructions reference](/instructions-reference).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste at the end of your first message">
    Add a blank line after your roleplay opening, then paste the instruction. Send the message normally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue the conversation naturally">
    All subsequent messages require no changes. The instruction persists in context automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  These instructions work in DeepSeek expert mode (web/app) and with the `deepseek-v4-flash` and `deepseek-v4-pro` APIs. Quick mode on the web does not support them.
</Note>
